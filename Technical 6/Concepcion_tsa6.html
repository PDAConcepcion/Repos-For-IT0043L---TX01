Finn

Finn Mertens, also known as Finn the Human, is the main protagonist in Adventure Time. He was adopted as an infant by two anthropomorphic dogs, Joshua and Margaret, who found him in the woods. His best friend and adoptive brother, Jake the Dog, accompanies him on many adventures in Ooo.

Jake

Jake, also known as Jacob "Jake" the Dog, Sr., is the deuteragonist of Adventure Time. He is a dog/shape-shifter hybrid, often referred to as a "magical dog." Jake is Finn's constant companion, best friend, and adoptive brother.

Princess Bubblegum

Princess Bonnibel "Bonnie" Bubblegum, often called PB, Peebles, Bub-Bubs, or P-Bubs, is one of the main characters in Adventure Time, first appearing in the animated short.

BMO

BMO, abbreviated from "Be MOre" and phonetically spelled "Beemo," is one of the main characters in Adventure Time and the titular protagonist of the Distant Lands episode of the same name. BMO is also referred to as Moe Mastro "Llabtoof" Giovanni Jr. and called the "New King of Ooo" in the future.

Ice King

Simon Petrikov, formerly known as the Ice King, is a major character in Adventure Time and a protagonist in its spin-off series, Adventure Time: Fionna & Cake. Initially depicted as a humorously over-the-top villain, his character was later revealed to be a kind-hearted but misunderstood old man with twisted senses of morality and social behavior, suffering from loneliness and memory loss.
